<p><strong>The Industrial Auction Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reserved Auction</li><li>Unreserved Auction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Auction Services operate primarily within two market types: Reserved Auctions and Unreserved Auctions. Reserved Auctions set a minimum price, allowing sellers to retain control over the sale outcome, as items won't sell unless bids meet or exceed this threshold. In contrast, Unreserved Auctions feature no minimum price, enabling all items to be sold regardless of bid amounts, often attracting more buyers. Each type caters to different seller strategies and market dynamics, influencing pricing and buyer participation.</p></p>
