<p><p>The anthracite coal market primarily consists of two types: lump anthracite and anthracite fines. Lump anthracite refers to larger, solid pieces of high-carbon coal, valued for their high heat output and low impurities, making them suitable for residential heating and industrial applications. Anthracite fines, on the other hand, are smaller, powdered particles often used in filtration, metal production, and as a fuel source in various industrial processes. Both types cater to specific needs within energy generation and industrial applications.</p></p>
