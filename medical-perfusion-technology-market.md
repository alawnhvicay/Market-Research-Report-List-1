<p><ul><li>Hypothermic Machine Perfusion</li><li>Normothermic Machine Perfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Perfusion Technology market is primarily divided into two types: Hypothermic Machine Perfusion and Normothermic Machine Perfusion. Hypothermic Machine Perfusion involves cooling organs to slow metabolic processes during preservation, enhancing viability for transplantation. In contrast, Normothermic Machine Perfusion maintains organs at normal body temperature, allowing for more effective assessment and preservation through continuous circulation and oxygenation of the organ. Both approaches aim to improve transplant outcomes and organ longevity, catering to the evolving needs of transplantation services.</p></p>
