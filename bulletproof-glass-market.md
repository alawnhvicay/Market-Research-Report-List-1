<p><p>Bulletproof glass, also known as ballistic glass, is a type of strong and durable material designed to withstand bullets and other projectiles. The demand for bulletproof glass is increasing due to the rising concerns over security and safety in both residential and commercial applications.</p><p>The Bulletproof Glass Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period. Technological advancements are also leading to improved materials offering better clarity and resistance, attracting a wider customer base. Moreover, the integration of bulletproof glass in luxury vehicles and high-end buildings reflects an evolving market landscape that aligns with heightened security awareness and architectural innovation.
