<p><p>Corporate Performance Management (CPM) systems can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CPM systems offer flexibility and scalability, enabling organizations to access performance data and analytics from anywhere with internet connectivity. This model reduces IT overhead and allows for easier updates. Conversely, on-premises CPM solutions are installed locally within an organization's infrastructure, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant investment in hardware and maintenance resources. Each option caters to different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
