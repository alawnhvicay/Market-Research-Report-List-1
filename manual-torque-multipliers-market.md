<p><strong>The Manual Torque Multipliers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reaction Bar Multiplier</li><li>Reaction Plate Multiplier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual torque multipliers market is primarily divided into two types: reaction bar multipliers and reaction plate multipliers. Reaction bar multipliers utilize a fixed bar to absorb the reaction torque, allowing for higher torque application without exerting excessive effort. In contrast, reaction plate multipliers employ a flat plate that distributes the reaction force, enhancing control and stability during operation. Both types are essential for various industrial applications, ensuring precise torque adjustment in maintenance and assembly tasks.</p></p>
