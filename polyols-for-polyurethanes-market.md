<p><strong>The Polyols for Polyurethanes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyether Polyols</li><li>Polyester Polyols</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyols for polyurethanes market is primarily divided into two types: polyether polyols and polyester polyols. Polyether polyols are synthesized from alkylene oxides and are known for their excellent low-temperature flexibility and resistance to moisture, making them ideal for flexible foam applications. In contrast, polyester polyols are derived from diols and dicarboxylic acids, offering superior chemical resistance and durability, which is beneficial for rigid foam and elastomer applications. Both types play crucial roles in various industrial and consumer products.</p></p>
