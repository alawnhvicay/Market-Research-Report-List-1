<p><p>The desalination market primarily utilizes two pump types: centrifugal and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps are widely used for their ability to handle large volumes of water efficiently, making them ideal for seawater intake and brine discharge. In contrast, positive displacement pumps are favored for applications requiring high pressure, such as reverse osmosis systems, ensuring consistent flow regardless of the system's resistance. Both pump types play crucial roles in enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of desalination processes.</p></p>
