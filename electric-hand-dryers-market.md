<p><p>The electric hand dryers market primarily consists of two types: jet air dryers and hot air dryers. Jet air dryers use high-speed airflow to remove moisture quickly, providing a more hygienic and efficient option. In contrast, hot air dryers utilize heated air to evaporate water, generally taking longer to dry hands. The choice between these types often depends on factors like installation cost, energy efficiency, drying speed, and hygiene requirements, impacting their adoption in various commercial restrooms.</p></p>
