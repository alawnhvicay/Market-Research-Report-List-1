The market includes peripheral stent delivery systems, designed for use in arteries outside the heart, such as those in the limbs. Vascular stent delivery systems focus on coronary arteries and other major vessels, ensuring proper placement of stents to prevent restenosis and improve blood flow. Both segments cater to specific clinical needs and are integral to interventional procedures.</p></p>
