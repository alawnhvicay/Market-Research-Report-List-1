<p><p>The aircraft main landing gear market is categorized into three primary types: rocker landing gear, pontoon landing gear, and framed landing gear. Rocker landing gear features a pivoting design, enhancing stability during landing and takeoff. Pontoon landing gear is specifically designed for amphibious aircraft, allowing for land and water operations. Framed landing gear consists of a robust framework that supports the aircraft, providing strength and stability. Each type serves unique operational needs in aviation, catering to various aircraft designs and environments.</p></p>
