<p><p>The instant beverage premix market encompasses three primary types: powder, paste, and granules. Powdered premixes are dry formulations that dissolve easily in hot or cold water, ideal for a wide range of beverages. Paste premixes offer a concentrated form, typically requiring dilution, and provide a rich flavor and texture, often used in specialty drinks. Granules feature coarser particles that require specific dissolution conditions, commonly found in coffee and tea products, appealing to consumers seeking convenience and speed in preparation.</p></p>
