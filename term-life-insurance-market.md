<p><p>Term life insurance is categorized into different market types based on their features. Renewable term life insurance allows policyholders to extend coverage at the end of the term without additional medical exams, providing flexibility as needs change. Return premium term life insurance offers a refund of premiums if the insured survives the policy term, making it an attractive option for those seeking both protection and a savings component. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences for security and value.</p></p>
