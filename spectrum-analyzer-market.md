<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Spectrum Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swept-Tuned</li><li>Vector Signal</li><li>Real-Time</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spectrum analyzers are categorized into three main types: Swept-Tuned, Vector Signal, and Real-Time analyzers. Swept-Tuned analyzers measure signal frequency characteristics by sweeping across a frequency range, providing a detailed view but with limited real-time capabilities. Vector Signal analyzers offer extensive modulation measurement and demodulation analysis, capturing both amplitude and phase information. Real-Time analyzers provide continuous monitoring of frequency components, allowing for the detection of transient events and ensuring no signal is missed, delivering immediate insights into signal behavior.</p></p>
