<p><p>Fiber optic extensometers are advanced sensors used to measure displacement and deformation in structures. The market can be categorized into two main types: Contact-Type Extensometers, which involve direct attachment to the material being monitored, allowing for precise measurement of strain; and Non Contact-Type Extensometers, which utilize laser or other optical methods to gauge deformation from a distance, minimizing potential interference with the material. Both types are crucial in civil engineering, geotechnical applications, and structural health monitoring.</p></p>
