<p><p>The high-end inertial sensors market encompasses various types, each serving unique applications. Accelerometers measure linear acceleration, enabling motion detection and vibration analysis. Gyroscopes provide orientation and angular velocity data, crucial for navigation and stabilization. Magnetometers detect magnetic fields, aiding in compass functionality and geo-mapping. GNSS receivers facilitate precise positioning and navigation by utilizing satellite signals. Other categories may include sensor fusion technologies and hybrid systems, enhancing performance and functionality in aerospace, automotive, robotics, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
