<p><p>The Offline X-Ray Inspection System market comprises two primary types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). Digital Radiography utilizes digital detectors to produce high-resolution images for quick analysis, making it ideal for various industrial applications. In contrast, Computed Tomography employs advanced algorithms to generate 3D images from multiple 2D X-ray images, providing detailed insights into an object's internal structure. Both technologies enhance inspection accuracy and efficiency, catering to diverse needs in quality control and safety assessment in industries.</p></p>
