<p><p>The Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD) equipment market is categorized mainly into two types: plate type and tubular type. Plate type PECVD systems utilize flat surfaces to create uniform coatings on substrates, making them ideal for large-area applications in electronics and solar cells. Tubular type systems, on the other hand, feature cylindrical reactors that allow for continuous processing and are suitable for coating cylindrical objects. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the specific requirements of the deposition process.</p></p>
