<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Centrifugal Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow Pumps</li><li>Mixed or Radial Flow Pumps</li><li>Peripheral Pumps</li><li>Jet Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The centrifugal pump market includes various types catering to diverse applications. Axial flow pumps move fluid along the pump axis, ideal for high-flow, low-head requirements. Mixed or radial flow pumps combine axial and radial flows, suitable for moderate flow and head capabilities. Peripheral pumps, or regenerative pumps, offer high head at low flow rates, making them efficient for small applications. Jet pumps utilize a jet of fluid to create suction, often used in water and sewage systems for optimal fluid transfer solutions.</p></p>
