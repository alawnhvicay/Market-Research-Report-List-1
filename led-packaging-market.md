<p><strong>The LED Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Packaging</li><li>COB Packaging</li><li>CSP Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED packaging market encompasses various types designed to improve performance and efficiency. SMD (Surface Mount Device) packaging involves mounting devices directly onto circuit boards, allowing for compact designs. COB (Chip-On-Board) packaging places multiple chips directly on a substrate, enhancing heat dissipation and light output. CSP (Chip Scale Package) is a miniaturized packaging option that reduces size while maintaining performance, making it ideal for high-density applications. Each type caters to specific requirements in LED technology and applications.</p></p>
