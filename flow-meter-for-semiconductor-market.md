<p><p>The semiconductor market for flow meters is primarily divided into gas flow meters and fluid flow meters. Gas flow meters are designed to monitor and control the flow of gases used in semiconductor manufacturing processes, such as nitrogen and argon. In contrast, fluid flow meters are utilized to measure the flow of liquids, including chemicals and cooling fluids critical for fabrication. Both types are essential for maintaining precise process control, ensuring efficiency, and enhancing yield in semiconductor production.</p></p>
