Wet etching employs liquid chemical solutions to remove materials, offering simplicity and uniformity, while dry etching utilizes plasma or gases for more precise and controlled etching, allowing for complex geometries in advanced semiconductor devices. Both types are crucial for achieving desired device performance.</p></p>
