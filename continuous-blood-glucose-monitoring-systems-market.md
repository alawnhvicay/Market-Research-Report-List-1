<p><p>The Continuous Blood Glucose Monitoring Systems market is primarily divided into two types: Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose Systems (SMBG) and Continuous Glucose Monitoring Systems (CGM). SMBG involves patients regularly checking their glucose levels using portable devices, providing snapshot readings. In contrast, CGMs offer real-time monitoring through sensors implanted under the skin, continually measuring glucose levels throughout the day. This seamless tracking helps patients manage diabetes more effectively, enabling timely adjustments in therapy and lifestyle choices to maintain optimal glucose control.</p></p>
