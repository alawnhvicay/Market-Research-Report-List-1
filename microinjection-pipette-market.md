<p><p>The microinjection pipette market comprises three main types: spiked tip, blunt tip, and beveled tip. Spiked tip pipettes feature a sharp end for precise penetration, ideal for injecting substances into single cells. Blunt tip pipettes have a rounded end, minimizing cellular damage, making them suitable for viscous solutions or delicate structures. Beveled tip pipettes, with an angled edge, offer a balance between precision and reduced trauma, catering to various applications in cell biology, embryology, and genetic engineering.</p></p>
