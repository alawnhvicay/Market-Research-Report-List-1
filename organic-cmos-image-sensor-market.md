<p><p>The organic CMOS image sensor market is segmented into two main types: linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture light along a single line, making them ideal for applications like barcode scanning and document scanning. In contrast, area image sensors capture a full image in one shot, suitable for cameras and imaging devices. Both types benefit from organic materials, offering advantages like improved sensitivity, lower power consumption, and enhanced image quality in various applications, including consumer electronics and industrial uses.</p></p>
