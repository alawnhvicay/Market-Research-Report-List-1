<p><p>The Gel-Filled Fiber Cable market is categorized into Single-Mode and Multi-Mode cables. Single-Mode cables use a single light path and are ideal for long-distance telecommunications, offering higher bandwidth and reduced signal loss. In contrast, Multi-Mode cables contain multiple light paths, making them suitable for shorter distances, such as within buildings or local networks, where high data rates are required over relatively short spans. Both types feature gel-filled designs to enhance protection against moisture and environmental factors.</p></p>
