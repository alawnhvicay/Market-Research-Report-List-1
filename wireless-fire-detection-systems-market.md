<p><ul><li>Addressable Wireless Fire Detection Systems</li><li>Conventional Wireless Fire Detection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wireless Fire Detection Systems market is primarily categorized into two types: Addressable Wireless Fire Detection Systems and Conventional Wireless Fire Detection Systems. Addressable systems allow for precise identification of the location of a fire, enabling efficient response and management. They communicate wirelessly, making installation simpler. In contrast, Conventional Wireless Fire Detection Systems operate on a simpler alarm triggering mechanism without specific location identification, making them suitable for smaller or less complex environments where comprehensive detection is not critical.</p></p>
