<p><p>Roller thrust bearings are specialized bearings designed to handle axial loads and provide high axial stiffness. They consist of cylindrical rollers positioned between two rings, allowing for efficient load distribution and reduced friction. These bearings are commonly used in applications requiring precise axial positioning, such as in automotive transmissions, gearboxes, and heavy machinery.</p><p>The Roller Thrust Bearings Market is poised for significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period.
