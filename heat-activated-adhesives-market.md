<p><p>The heat-activated adhesives market consists primarily of two types: tape and film. Heat-activated adhesive tapes are designed to bond surfaces upon the application of heat, providing strong, durable connections ideal for various industrial applications. In contrast, heat-activated adhesive films offer a flexible bonding solution, suitable for complex shapes and surfaces, commonly used in textiles, laminates, and electronics. Both types leverage temperature to create optimum adhesion, enhancing efficiency and reliability in manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
