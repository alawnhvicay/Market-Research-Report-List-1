<p><p>The Animal Anti-Rabies Vaccine market is primarily segmented into pre-exposure and post-exposure prophylaxis applications. Pre-exposure prophylaxis is aimed at high-risk groups, such as veterinarians and animal handlers, to provide preventive immunity against rabies. Conversely, post-exposure prophylaxis is administered following potential rabies exposure, typically through animal bites, to prevent the onset of the disease. Both applications are vital in controlling rabies transmission and ensuring public health safety, thereby driving demand in the vaccine market.</p></p>
