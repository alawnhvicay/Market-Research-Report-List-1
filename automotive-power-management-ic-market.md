<p><ul><li>Discrete Type</li><li>Highly Integrated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Power Management IC (PMIC) market is primarily divided into two types: Discrete Type and Highly Integrated Type. Discrete Type PMICs consist of individual components designed for specific functions, offering flexibility in circuit design but requiring more space and complexity. In contrast, Highly Integrated Type PMICs combine multiple functions into a single chip, optimizing space and efficiency while simplifying design. Both types cater to the rising demand for efficient power management in various automotive applications, particularly in electrification trends.</p></p>
