<p><p>The wood-flooring adhesives market comprises three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and urethane-based adhesives. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly, offering low VOC levels and easy cleanup, making them popular for residential applications. Solvent-based adhesives provide strong bonding and durability but emit higher VOCs, suitable for commercial settings. Urethane-based adhesives are known for their superior flexibility and moisture resistance, making them ideal for challenging environments. Each type serves distinct needs based on application, performance, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
