Catalase helps eliminate hydrogen peroxide used in bleaching processes, ensuring fabric integrity. Pectinase assists in the processing of natural fibers, while laccase is involved in dye degradation and finishing. Other enzymes in this market address specialized textile needs, promoting innovation and sustainability in the industry.</p></p>
