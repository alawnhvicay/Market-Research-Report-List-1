<p><p>The arsenic removal market comprises several key types of processes. The precipitative process involves chemical reactions that form insoluble compounds, facilitating solid removal. Adsorptive processes utilize materials to capture arsenic ions, enhancing water quality. Ion exchange processes involve swapping arsenic ions with less harmful ones in a resin medium. Membrane processes employ filters to physically separate arsenic from water. Other methods may include advanced technologies, biological processes, and hybrid systems, each addressing arsenic contamination challenges effectively.</p></p>
